About Us

About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO dedicated to education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare and youth development.

Section 8 NGO
80G & 12A Certified
CSR-1 Registered
NITI Aayog Registered
ISO 9001:2015
